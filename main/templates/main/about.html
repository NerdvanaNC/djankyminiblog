DjankyMiniblog

A public mini blog where people can post anything.

Stories, letters, thoughts, journal entries, jokes, anything. Posts have a once-a-day limit, and require an account. Works just like a personal blog, WYSIWYG with a generous character limit.

Not trying to replace Reddit, just wanted a practice project. I feel like this is a great proof-of-concept for a place where people can have a public journal. Since this is strictly text-only, the experience is akin to reading letters.

Pretty sure this'll just devolve into ASCII dicks if it ever really goes out into the world, but a very fun project nonetheless; I learnt a lot!
